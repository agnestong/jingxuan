From the Streets to Olympics

This is a personal project where I challenged myself to create a printed poster design. The theme of this poster is about Breaking (commercially known as Breakdance), Breaking has always been an underground dance genre that is part of the Hip-Hop culture since the beginnning of Hip-Hop history, and recently, Breaking was officially announced to be a part of 2024 Paris Olympics. Though there were many debates within the scene on this news, I was happy with this announcement nonetheless! Hence, I decided to create a typography based poster expressing how happy and proud I feel about the progress Breaking has made. The poster is titled "From the Streets to Olympics", where I try to design a graffiti typeface to represent the Hip-Hop spirit in the Breaking scene.
